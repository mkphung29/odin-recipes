Madison's Classic Turkey Club Sandwich

Description

Learn how to make Madison's favorite sandwich: the ultimate turkey club! It's a classic that's super easy to make and super delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (whichever you prefer)
  • 4 slices of oven roasted turkey
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 2 slices of American cheese
  • 4 slices of tomato
  • 1 slice of lettuce
  • 2 strips of bacon
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • 4 slices of pickles
  • 1 whole dill pickle
  • Butter
  • 1 tbs of olive oil

Steps

  1. Slice the tomatoes, lettuce, and cheese (if you have to)
  2. Spread butter on each slice of bread and toast them for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Heat up a pan with the olive oil. Toss in the bacon until thoroughly cooked then let it sit.
  4. After the bread is toasted, spread ketchup on one slice and mayo and mustard on the other slice.
  5. On the ketchup slice, add the cheddar and American cheeses.
  6. On the mayo slice, add the slices of turkey by folding them over to fit.
  7. Add the lettuce on top of the cheese and the tomatoes on top of the turkey. Put the pickles on top of the tomatoes.
  8. Add the bacon on top of the pickles.
  9. Put the sandwich together, serve with the dill pickle, and you're done!
