Fried Rice

Description

This is how you make Benihana style fried rice. This version has been perfected by Madison Phung.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 cups of fried rice (preferably a day old at least)
  • Half a cup of chopped yellow onions
  • 1 tbs of garlic
  • 1 cup of chopped green onions
  • 3-4 eggs
  • 2 tbs of soy sauce
  • 2 tbs of sesame oil
  • 2 tbs of butter
  • 1 tbs of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Chop the yellow onions, garlic, and green onions if you haven't already done so.
  2. Crack the eggs in a bowel and beat. Add a touch of salt and pepper.
  3. Heat up a pan on medium heat and pour in olive oil. Let the oil heat up, then add 1 tbs of butter, the yellow onions, and the garlic. Heat until golden brown.
  4. Set the onions and garlic to one side of the pan. Next, add the beat eggs on the other side. Scramble the eggs
  5. Add in the rice and mix everything together. Once everything is thoroughly mixed, add in the green onions, 1 tbs of butter, 2 tbs of soy sauce, 2 tbs of sesame oil, and salt and pepper. Mix.
  6. Mix in the cup of green onions. Add more soy sauce, sesame oil, and salt and pepper to your taste.
  7. Enjoy!
